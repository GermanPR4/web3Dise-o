Fearless Speed

Extreme Moto Athletes

These elite riders have conquered dirt, air, and time itself. With every jump, twist, and victory, they push the boundaries of what's possible on two wheels—fueling a movement that blends passion, risk, and raw talent.

    • Extreme Riders, Pure Adrenaline

    From jaw-dropping stunts to brutal terrain, discover the icons who redefine the limits of extreme motorcycling.

    Travis Pastrana

    Considered one of the most extreme motorsport athletes, Travis Pastrana has redefined FMX. He was the first to land a double backflip in competition and is the mastermind behind Nitro Circus. With an always radical approach, he has also competed in rally and pulled off impossible stunts.

    Graham Jarvis

    A hard enduro legend, Graham dominates races like Erzbergrodeo and Romaniacs. His technical and quiet style has earned him worldwide respect on the most extreme terrains on the planet.

    Tom Pagès

    A freestyle innovator, Tom is known for tricks like the bike flip and front flair. He blends technique, risk, and creativity in every jump — an artist in the air on two wheels.
